FAQ

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Statistics from the main menu, then tap "Delete my data". This will reset all stats, unlocked levels, and saved chain progress.

The game runs slowly on my device

LUCID is designed to run smoothly on most Android devices. If you experience performance issues, try closing other apps running in the background. The game requires Android 7.1 (API 25) or higher.

How do I unlock new difficulty levels?

Win the previous level to unlock the next one. Beat RÊVE to unlock SONGE, beat SONGE to unlock LUCIDE. Chain Mode unlocks after your first victory on any level.

What is the Seed number?

Every board is generated from a unique seed (shown as a hex code like #A3F2). Share it with friends so they can play the exact same puzzle and compare strategies.

How does Chain Mode work?

Chain Mode is an infinite gauntlet of escalating arenas. You play RÊVE, then SONGE, then LUCIDE, then increasingly harder variants. One defeat ends your run. Your score accumulates across all arenas.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No. LUCID works 100% offline. No accounts, no ads, no data is ever sent anywhere. All your progress is stored locally on your device.

How do I change the language?

During a game, tap the pause button (II), then tap the FR/EN toggle to switch between French and English.

I purchased the premium features but they're not showing

Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, make sure your Google Play purchase was completed successfully by checking your purchase history in the Google Play app. Contact us if you need further help.

Tip: The tutorials teach you everything you need to know. If you're stuck, try replaying the lesson about the specific mechanic that's giving you trouble (Rift retaliation, chain reactions, or lucidity management).
