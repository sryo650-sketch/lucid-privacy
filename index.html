How It Works

Four core mechanics. Infinite strategic depth.

◎

Nodes

Activate to stabilize neighbors. Move to reposition. Sacrifice to lock down threats — at a cost.

⯉

Catalysts

Single-use powerhouses. Invert the state of all adjacent cells. High risk, high reward.

◆

The Rift

The source of chaos. Indestructible. Retaliates when you stabilize nearby. Contain it to win.

◉

Lucidity

Every action costs Lucidity. Run out and your mind collapses. Efficient play earns bonuses.
