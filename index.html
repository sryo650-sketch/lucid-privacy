Features

A pure tactical puzzle — no filler, no timers, no ads. Just you against the grid.

Hex Grid Tactics

Every cell on the hexagonal grid matters. Activate, move, or sacrifice Nodes to stabilize your surroundings and push back the entropy.

The Rift Fights Back

The Rift retaliates against every move near it. Each stabilization triggers a counter-attack. Outsmart it or be overwhelmed.

Chain Reactions

Catalysts invert their neighbors, stressed Nodes cascade instability. Master the chain reactions to turn chaos into order.

Synthwave Soundtrack

Handcrafted procedural audio and synthwave music immerse you in a neon cyberpunk dreamscape.

100% Offline

No accounts. No ads. No data collection. No internet required. Your game, your device, your privacy.

Seed Replay

Every board has a unique seed. Share it with friends to challenge them on the exact same puzzle.

Daily Challenge

Three fresh puzzles every day — one per difficulty. Track your streak, share your results, and compare with friends.

Achievements

100 badges to unlock across all modes: speedruns, economy plays, daily streaks, chain records, and mastery milestones.

Native Sharing

Share your results with one tap. The native share sheet lets you send your score to any app — WhatsApp, Twitter, and more.

Colorblind Mode

Smart Hints

4 Guided Lessons

How It Works

Four core mechanics. Infinite strategic depth.

Nodes

Activate to stabilize neighbors. Move to reposition. Sacrifice to lock down threats — at a cost.

Catalysts

Single-use powerhouses. Invert the state of all adjacent cells. High risk, high reward.

The Rift

The source of chaos. Indestructible. Retaliates when you stabilize nearby. Contain it to win.

Lucidity

Every action costs Lucidity. Run out and your mind collapses. Efficient play earns bonuses.
